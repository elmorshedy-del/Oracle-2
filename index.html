How it works: The bot runs 4 modes simultaneously. Mode 1 (Quiet) places symmetric buy/sell orders on both sides, earning the spread + maker rebates. Mode 2 (Lean) activates when the BTC feed shows momentum — quotes shift to favor the predicted direction. Mode 3 (Event) fires on breaking news (LLM-assessed) or extreme BTC moves — aggressive one-sided orders. Mode 4 (Arb) triggers when Kalshi and Polymarket price the same event differently — locks in cross-platform profit. Mode 1 is always running; the others modify its behavior.
